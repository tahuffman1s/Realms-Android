What changed

  • Narrator chrome gone. No header row, no icon, no chevron, no surrounding bubble. Replaced by a subtle left rail. NarrationBlock.kt:210–234
  • Turn divider retired to a 40dp centered hairline gap. ChatFeed.kt:164–187
  • Bubble labels dropped. NPC name inlines with the first word of the quote; player bubble has no label — right-alignment already signals ownership. MessageBubbles.kt:142–153
  • Avatars removed from feed. NPC identity carried by a 6px leading dot + inline name in accent color. Player identity carried by right-alignment. MessageBubbles.kt:91, 163–184
  • Stat pills collapsed to a single muted dot-separated strip. Red for losses, green for gains, no emoji. Overflow hides behind a tap. MessageBubbles.kt:229–328
  • Action pills unified to inline italic lines with a tiny colored leading dot (NPC action / aside / player action). Same space, fraction of the weight. NarrationBlock.kt:467–516
  • Check results inlined as a leading chip on the stat strip instead of a center-row Surface. MessageBubbles.kt:477–499
  • Past turns dimmed to 55% — the latest beat is the brightest thing on screen.
  • Merchant chips moved from inline-after-turn to a persistent footer dock above the input. ChatFeed.kt:101–127

What stayed

  • Per-NPC accent colors (via the 6px dot and the inline name).
  • Italic for all dialogue/action text.
  • Player bubble right-alignment.
  • Check PASS/FAIL legibility (still color-coded, just attached).

Taste calls still open

  • Avatars: gone entirely, or keep a 20dp version on the latest turn only?
  • Turn gap: silent hairline (shown) vs. tiny "T7" superscript for long-session wayfinding?
  • Past-turn dimming: on by default vs. only when the feed exceeds N turns?
  • Stat strip: current dot-separated text, vs. emoji-only (no words) to shave another pass of visual noise?
